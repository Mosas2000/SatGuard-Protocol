# SatGuard Protocol - User Guide

## Welcome to SatGuard

SatGuard is a Bitcoin-backed micro-insurance protocol that allows you to pool sBTC with others to insure against crypto-related risks.

## Getting Started

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

- Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
- Choose your Stacks wallet (Hiro or Xverse)
- Approve the connection

### 2. Browse Insurance Pools

- View active insurance pools on the "Browse Pools" tab
- Each pool shows:
  - Coverage type (e.g., Exchange Hack Protection)
  - Total funds pooled
  - Number of contributors
  - Minimum contribution required
  - Maximum coverage amount

### 3. Create a Pool

- Switch to the "Create Pool" tab
- Fill in the details:
  - **Coverage Type**: What risk does this pool insure against?
  - **Minimum Contribution**: Smallest amount users can contribute
  - **Maximum Coverage**: Largest claim that can be paid out
- Submit the transaction

### 4. Contribute to a Pool

- Select a pool you want to join
- Enter your contribution amount (must meet minimum)
- Confirm the transaction
- Your contribution gives you voting power

### 5. Submit a Claim

- If you experience a covered loss, submit a claim
- Provide:
  - Pool ID
  - Claim amount (up to max coverage)
  - Detailed reason for the claim
- Your claim will be voted on by pool members

### 6. Vote on Claims

- Review pending claims
- Vote to approve or reject based on legitimacy
- Your voting power equals your contribution amount
- Claims need 60% approval and 50% participation

## Security Tips

- Only contribute what you can afford to lose
- Verify pool details before contributing
- Vote responsibly on claims
- Keep your wallet secure
